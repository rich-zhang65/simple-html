Commercial Marine Transport Services in the Heart of Vancouver

Guiding you on the water. To get your job done.

Moored in Downtown Vancouver, our diverse fleet and a lifetime of experience on our citys coastline ensure your crew get on and off the water safely and efficiently - whatever your goals may be.

Our Services

On-site and secure parking along False Creek for simple and rapid access to Vancouvers coastal waters

  • Providing at sea support for film production in Vancouver. Our experienced team and diverse fleet of vessels provide reliable transportation and a versatile platform for creative shoots and water-access location scouts.

  • Local knowledge and sampling equipment to guide your field work (with major deck space and heated cabins to keep you and your crew happy!).

    With sustainable development at heart, let us support your crews next at-sea project in a wide array of activities:

    Marine Sediment Sampling (suite of Van Veen, Ponar and Petite Ponar grab samplers available)

    Seawater Sampling

    Marine Environmental Impact and Assessments

    Disposal at Sea and/or Contaminated Site Assessments

    Fisheries Studies (stock assessments, biodiversity, and habitat management)

    Marine Botany and Biology

    Marine Environment Clean-Up Assistance

    and more

  • Our Transport Canada-certified vessels can safely transport crew and equipment to your water-access sites. Smooth and on-time operations building success on the water.

  • Do you have a special project or request? Contact our team to discuss the marine logistics and details of your next job.
